## Formative Assessment

For your final project, you will be required to use satellite data to assess land use change over a region and topic of your choice. 

Take some time now to think about topics that may be of interest to you. You may have a good idea of an event of interest. A few examples might include:

 - Wildfires and deforestation.
 - The retreat of glaciers and ice caps.
 - Urbanisation.
 - Human influence on the landscape (afforestation, the creation/destruction of reservoirs, etc.).
 - The impact of drought years

A good tool to explore preliminary changes is the Google Earth Timelapse tool, available at [https://earthengine.google.com/timelapse/](https://earthengine.google.com/timelapse/).
